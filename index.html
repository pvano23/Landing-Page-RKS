WHO AM I?

HELLO! MY NAME IS REN KOK

MY EXPERIENCE

  • Scouting Academy Graduate
  • Former special teams coach with the Cambridge Lions
  • Former high school QB/WR coach

My ultimate goal is to become a professional football scout. As part of that journey, I focus on scouting high school football players. I've been mentored by former NFL scouts and continue to learn from coaches at all levels to sharpen my skills. In addition to scouting, I have experience as a high school coach, having worked with quarterbacks and kickers/punters, including developing a recent OSFL All-Star kicker. I keep my costs low for athletes because I believe in supporting their journey without breaking the bank. I have my own goals to reach, and I'm committed to helping others achieve theirs along the way!
